# ✉️ Email Builder — Visual Editor

A powerful, completely free-form visual email editor designed to create stunning, modern, and responsive email templates directly in your browser. With a drag-and-drop interface and dozens of built-in components, you can craft beautiful emails and instantly export them as Gmail-ready, responsive HTML.

## ✨ Features

- **Free-form Canvas**: Drag, resize, and freely position blocks anywhere on the canvas. Say goodbye to rigid grid restrictions.
- **15 Unique Block Types**: Includes Text, Image, Button, Hero, Columns, 3-Columns, Video, Quote, List, Countdown, Footer, Header, Divider, Spacer, and Social Links.
- **15 Professional Templates**: Pre-designed, one-click templates for Welcome Emails, Promos, Newsletters, Onboarding, Product Launches, Event Invites, Feedback, Abandoned Cart, and more.
- **15 Stunning CSS Effects**: Elevate your designs with built-in effects like Drop Shadow, Neon Outline, Glassmorphism, Fade In, Scale Pulse, Float, Bounce In, and even a Typewriter effect.
- **Gmail-Compatible HTML Export**: One-click export translates your absolute-positioned, visually designed blocks into table-based, fully responsive HTML that renders perfectly across major email clients (like Gmail, Outlook, and Apple Mail).
- **Responsive Previews**: Quickly toggle between Desktop and Mobile views to ensure your email looks perfect on any screen.
- **Snap-to-Grid**: Toggle the grid on or off to easily align your blocks or go completely freehand.
- **History Management**: Full Undo & Redo capabilities.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open the App**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2. **Add Blocks**: Click any component from the "Add Blocks" panel on the left to add it to your canvas.
3. **Position & Resize**: Drag the block to your desired location. Grab the bottom-right corner of a block when selected to resize its width.
4. **Customize**: Click on a block to open the Properties panel on the right. Edit text, change colors, adjust padding, set links, or add animation effects.
5. **Use Templates**: Need a head start? Click a template from the bottom-left to instantly load a full email layout.
6. **Export**: Once your masterpiece is ready, click **Copy HTML** or **Download** in the top-right toolbar. The tool handles the complex table-based HTML generation automatically.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

Built entirely with standard web technologies for maximum performance and portability:
- **HTML5** & **CSS3**
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (No external libraries or frameworks required)

## 📁 File Structure

- `index.html`: The core editor layout and UI structure.
- `styles.css`: The premium design system, dark mode, effects, animations, and canvas UI.
- `script.js`: The powerhouse behind drag-and-drop, state management, block rendering, and the Gmail-compatible HTML export engine.
